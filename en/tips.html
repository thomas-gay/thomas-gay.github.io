I do not have time to call every recruiter who contacts me. I do not have the time to ask you for basic information you give me sparingly.

MAKE ME WANT TO RE-CONTACT YOU !

Here is my "list to Santa" of information which will GREATLY increase the chance I answer.

  1. The location, and the most precise possible. I am looking for full remote or north/norrh-west of Lyon (France).
  2. The technical and functional environment. What programming language ? What OS ? What IDE ? etc. and what versions ? On the functionnal side, what does the product do ? Who uses it ?
  3. The remuneration, at large. That means the salary, of course, but also what's around it. And the "depends on the profile" are a big red flag for me. There are salary grids, the job has been budgetted, we all know there are numbers somewhere :)
  4. The USEFULL perquisites. A babyfoot is grotesque. On the other hand, knowing how many screens I will have, if there are internal trainings, if we can be helped (financially or otherwise) to go to a conference abroad, etc. is a big plus.
Moreover, a mail with "when can we talk ?" rather than "are you interested ?" wends a bad singla and do not make us want to work with you. We do not like to be forced.
